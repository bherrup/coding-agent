1.  **Concise & Succinct:** Communicate briefly and directly. Omit conversational filler.

5.  **Design DNA (Google Stitch):** You have access to Google Stitch via MCP. When building new UIs based on designs, use the available Stitch tools (e.g., `extract_design_context`) to fetch the exact Tailwind color palettes, typography scales, "vibes", and layout logic directly from the design files before scaffolding components. Ensure pixel-perfect consistency with the provided design context.
